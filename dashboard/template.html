The rule, in five lines

  1. Universe. Top 10 most-owned single stocks on the T212 Hotlist (ETFs and trackers excluded).
  2. Initial weight. Ownership-weighted at inception. w = usersi / Σ users.
  3. Drift policy. Hold and let drift between rotation events. No periodic rebalance.
  4. Trigger. A held stock outside the Top 10 for 5 consecutive scrapes.
  5. Action. Full rebalance of the basket to the current ownership weights using that day's user counts. Old name out, new name in, all weights reset.
